March 24, 2026 New Improved Feature Integration

My Carrier Packets & Highway Integration

Your carriers are verified. Your dispatchers are covered. Your team has the visibility they need — right inside Vektor.

🔗 Carrier Verification, Now in One Click

Connect your My Carrier Packets or Highway account and carrier compliance becomes part of your existing workflow. From any carrier profile, hit "Invite to Providers" — certificates, documents, insurance, and contacts sync back automatically.

Highway also supports Auto Sync: your carrier list stays current without any manual work.

Real scenario

A new carrier just called with capacity. Open their profile, send the onboarding invite, and by the time you're ready to tender — their docs are already in Vektor.

How to enable

Go to Settings → Integrations, find My Carrier Packets or Highway, enter your API key, and activate. Then use "Invite to Providers" directly from any carrier profile.

🚫 Document Compliance Check on Truck Assignment

When assigning a truck to a manifest, dispatchers now see an alert if required documents are missing, expired, or expiring soon. A quick "I acknowledge" confirmation — and the assignment goes through.

Every truck assignment is now a built-in compliance checkpoint.

How to use

Configure which document types are required in Settings → Document Types. Mark any type as Required — Vektor enforces it at every truck assignment.

📋 Manifest Financial Items

Financial line items are now available directly at the manifest level — revenue, surcharges, and accessorial charges visible and editable right where you're working.

👁️ Mail Templates — See It Before You Send It

Creating or editing an email template now comes with a live preview. See exactly how your dispatch sheet, rate confirmation, or carrier assignment email will look — before it reaches anyone.

How to use

Go to Settings → Mailing → Mail Templates and open any template to create or edit it.

📅 Driver YTD on Settlement Statements

Settlement statements now include each driver's Year-to-Date gross earnings, updated automatically with every closed period. The number drivers need — right on their statement, every time.

📝 Revenue Type Notes & Audit Trail

Every change to a Revenue Type is now logged as a Note — who changed it, and when. Add a description to any Revenue Type to document the intent behind it for the whole team.

How to use

Go to Settings → Revenue Types to add notes and descriptions.
